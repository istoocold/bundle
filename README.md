# Bundle - Your Stress-Free Break Companion



## What is Bundle?

**Bundle** is a Chrome extension that brings quick, engaging games right into your browser. Designed for busy people who need a mental break without leaving their workspace, Bundle lets you decompress in just a few minutes with fun sliding puzzles.

### Why Bundle?

- **Stress Relief** - Take short, refreshing breaks during your workday
- **No Context Switching** - Stay in your browser, no need to open separate apps
- **Quick Sessions** - Games designed for 2-5 minute mental breaks
- **Always Accessible** - Just one click away in your browser toolbar

---

## Featured Games

### Sliding Puzzle
Solve beautiful image puzzles in two difficulty modes:
- **3×3 Mode** - Classic 8-tile puzzle for quick breaks
- **4×4 Mode** - Challenging 15-tile puzzle for deeper focus

Look for the **67** icon marking the empty space, click adjacent tiles to slide them, and recreate the complete image.

## How to Install & Run

### Step 1: Download the Extension
Download Bundle from [**this link**](https://github.com/istoocold/bundle/archive/refs/heads/main.zip)

### Step 2: Extract the Files
- Unzip the downloaded file to a location on your computer
- You should see a folder structure with the extension files inside

### Step 3: Load in Chrome
1. Open **Chrome** browser
2. Navigate to `chrome://extensions/` (or go to **Menu > Extensions > Manage Extensions**)
3. Enable **Developer Mode** (toggle switch in the top-right corner)
4. Click **"Load unpacked"** button
5. Navigate to the extracted folder: `bundle > chrome_extension`
6. Select the `chrome_extension` folder and click **"Select Folder"**

### Step 4: Start Playing
- Look for the Bundle icon in your Chrome toolbar
- Click it to open the extension popup
- Choose your game and start destressing
