How to Play

  • Look for the empty tile marked with the 67 icon
  • Click on any tile adjacent to the 67 tile
  • The clicked tile will swap places with the 67 tile
  • Keep sliding tiles until the complete image is formed
  • Only tiles directly next to the empty space can be moved
